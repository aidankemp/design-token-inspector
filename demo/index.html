Our Story

Founded in 1974, Metropolitan Theater Company has been the heartbeat of our city's cultural landscape for over five decades. What began as a small community theater has grown into one of the region's most respected performing arts organizations.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to create transformative theatrical experiences that challenge, inspire, and unite our community. We believe in the power of live performance to spark conversations, evoke emotions, and bridge divides between people from all walks of life.

From intimate black box productions to grand mainstage spectacles, we've produced over 400 shows, launched countless careers, and touched the lives of more than half a million audience members. Our commitment to artistic excellence, educational outreach, and community engagement remains unwavering.

About Metropolitan Theater Company

For over 50 years, Metropolitan Theater Company has been at the forefront of live performance arts, bringing world-class productions to our community. We believe in the transformative power of theater to inspire, challenge, and unite audiences from all walks of life. Our mission is to create extraordinary theatrical experiences that celebrate the human spirit and explore the depths of storytelling through innovative productions, talented artists, and meaningful community engagement.
